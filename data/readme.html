Foursquare processes your personal information:

  • to operate, support, and improve the Consumer Services you request, purchase, or engage with,
  • to personalize your experience with our Consumer Services,
  • to conduct ordinary business operations, verifying your identity, conducting business research, auditing, analytics, planning and strategy, corporate reporting, and management,
  • to communicate with you about our Consumer Services and providing information required by law or applicable corporate updates related to mergers, acquisitions, or divestitures, and
  • to conduct data analytics and use automated processing, artificial intelligence and machine learning to support internal research, create new products and services, improve functionality of existing products and services, and develop new features such as those developed to enhance security and protect privacy.

The information processed consists of contact data, security credentials & account data, demographic data, geolocation data, user generated content, online activity data and device identifier data.

Your personal information is processed by our Customer Support, Engineering and Product teams.

We also disclose personal information to third parties who process personal information on our behalf, such as cloud storage, IT services, marketing, analytics and search engine and other software services and to law enforcement agencies if we are required to do so or if we think this is necessary to protect the rights of others.

These service providers may be located in countries other than the country where the information originally was collected, such as the US and Canada. Where this is the case, we rely on your consent or other instruments to transfer this information. We do not authorize our service providers to retain, use or disclose the data except as necessary to perform services on our behalf or comply with legal requirements.

We keep the personal data you provide for the purposes indicated above for the duration of our relationship, plus a period of up to three (3) years. Foursquare may retain the information longer for our legitimate business purposes or to comply with the applicable statute of limitations periods and other applicable laws or legal considerations.

We do not carry any automated decision-making and only build profiles where permitted by and in accordance with law.

If you consider that any of the personal information should be corrected, or if you object to our processing of the data or want us to erase or restrict the way we process the personal information, please refer to ourYour Privacy Choices page for detailed instructions on the steps you can take. In some circumstances, these rights can be limited - for example if we are required by law to process personal information.

If you have a complaint about the way we have processed your personal information, you may contact us via ourPrivacy Portal.
